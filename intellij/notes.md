

# Common Changes That Made to Intellij

## Keymaps

- "Delete Line": ⌘D
- "Delete to Line Start": ⌘ Backspace
- "Duplicate Line or Selection": ⌘⇧C
- "Move Caret to Text End": ⌘↓
- "Move Caret to Text Start": ⌘↑
- "Move Line Down": ⌥↓
- "Move Line Up": ⌥↑
- "Reopen Closed Tab": ⌘⇧W
- "Search Everywhere": ⌘O
- "Sort Lines": ⌘⇧S
- "Indent Line or Selection": ⌘]
- "Unindent Line or Selection": ⌘[

diff in editor? ⌘⇧D
